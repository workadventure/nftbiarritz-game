Adventurer,

Welcome aboard the Galion ship! An exciting puzzle awaits you. You must discover the secret sentence made up of 6 words. Each fragment of the sentence is numerically encoded in objects present on the map. You must find the objects, assemble the sentence to try to win your Woka NFT 😍

Règles du Jeu :

🗺️ Find objets: 6 objets to discover to complete the secret sentence. Each object discovered allows to complete the secret sentence and to win the digital key.

🔍 The clues: throughout the game, clues will be given to you to help you find the objects of your quest.

🔐 End of game: once the secret sentence is completed, you will have to find the place to validate the end of the game. If you access the place before you have found all the items to complete the secret sentence, you will not be able to access the end of the game but clues will be given to you to continue searching.

🎁 Reward: end of game validated, you can register and try to win a Woka NFT.

Get ready to take up the challenge, to call on your skills and to discover the secrets hidden on board the Galion boat. Good luck, dear adventurers!
